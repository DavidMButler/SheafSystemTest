  Cameras

The cameras are displayed under the Cameras tab on the left hand side of the viewer. Right clicking a highlighted camera or set of highlighted cameras will open a menu to manipulate them.
  • New [Ctrl-N] will add a camera to the stage. The initial values of the camera will be determined by VTK.
  • Edit Camera(s) [Ctrl-E] will open a dialog for editing the highlighted camera(s). The dialog has a number of tabbed panels for manipulating them. After making the desired changes, Apply will apply the changes. Cancel will restore the settings back to the last applied values.
  • Remove Camera(s) [Ctrl-R] will remove the highlighted camera(s) from the stage.
  • Select Camera [Ctrl-S] will select the highlight camera. The selected camera is identified by bold red text. The stage is viewed by the selected camera.

The Camera menu on the top menu bar is a shortcut for the selected camera. Camera > Reset Camera will reset the camera to the values of the selected scene in the script. Camera > Auto View will set the camera to the VTK default position and focal point.
