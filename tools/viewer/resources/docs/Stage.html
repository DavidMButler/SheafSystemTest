  Stage

The stage is where the current scene is acted out. A scene is a collection of actors, lights and cameras. The stage is displayed through the viewport on the right hand side of the LPS Viewer. The viewport is a view of the stage through the selected camera.

Mousing around in the viewport will change the values of the selected camera. Mousing while pressing the left mouse button will rotate the camera within three dimensional space. Mousing while pressing the left mouse button and holding down the Ctrl key will rotate the camera within the two dimensional plane of the viewport. Mousing while pressing the right mouse button will zoom the camera.
