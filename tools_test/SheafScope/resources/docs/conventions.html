  Conventions

Bold words indicates clickable components in the user interface. Unqualified bold words refer to buttons or menu items. Other gui components will be qualified.

Examples:
  • Cancel
  • Remove Actor(s)
  • Actor tab
Bold words separated by a greater than sign identify a series of menu clicks starting at the menu bar and ending with a menu item.

Examples:
  • Actor > Reset Actor
  • Script > Save > Scene
Words within brackets identify keyboard shortcuts.

Example:
  • [Ctrl-N]
Important words or sentences will by italicized

Examples:
  • This must be done.
  • Note: This is really important.
